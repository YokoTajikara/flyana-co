Eligible Countries
Indonesia, Malaysia, Philippines, Singapore, Thailand and Vietnam.
Eligible Customers
  • Customers who purchase tickets on ANA operated flights with eligible itinerary during the campaign application period.
  • Customers who transit at Narita airport both outbound and inbound.
  • Customers who purchase tickets on other airlines flights or codeshare flights marketed by ANA operated by other airlines will NOT be eligible.
  • Customers who hold mileage redemption tickets will NOT be eligible.
Eligible Itinerary
  • The final destination of the itinerary must be any of cities in the United States of America.
  • Itinerary must be round-trip originating from one of the eligible countries.
  • All flights in the itinerary must be operated by ANA and all flight numbers must under ANA's flight numbers except domestic flights in the United States of America.
  • Transit airport in Tokyo must be Narita airport both outbound and inbound.
  • The itinerary with dates of transit (inbound and outbound) at Narita airport between December 2nd, 2016 - March 31st, 2017.
  • Departure date of the return flight from Narita to one of the eligible countries must be earlier than March 31st, 2017.
Remarks
  • If daily transfer usage exceeds 500MB, transfer speeds will be reduced.
  • If eligible customer rent WiFi router for more than seven days, US$13 for the VisionGlobalWiFi router rental fee will be applied each day on customers own expense.
  • One ticket number is eligible for one application only.
